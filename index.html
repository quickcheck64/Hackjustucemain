About Hacker Justice

Hacker Justice is a digital recovery firm with a clear mission — to restore hope and assets for victims of online fraud. Our team of professional white-hat hackers, blockchain experts, and legal analysts combine technology and strategy to trace stolen assets and hold scammers accountable.

Founded on principles of transparency, professionalism, and results, we’ve handled cases globally with a remarkable recovery rate. Whether you've been scammed through a fake investment site, lost crypto, or had your social media hijacked — Hacker Justice is your path to recovery.

How We Work

  • 1. Free Case Review: Submit your report. Our team evaluates feasibility and scope.
  • 2. Investigation Phase: We trace scammers, funds, and digital trails using forensic-grade software.
  • 3. Client Engagement: We report findings and begin the legal or strategic recovery path.
  • 4. Recovery Execution: Based on the scam type, we initiate wallet tracing, chargebacks, asset freezing, or identity restoration.
